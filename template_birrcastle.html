Birr Castle Demesne

This award winning castle presents you to explore a culmination of history, science, engineering, local trees, flowers and wildlife. Celebrating 400 years in 2021, this castle sits on romantic and scenic hills that overlook the green fields of Ireland.

Hidden Quirks

Birr Castle Demesne boasts a beautiful parkland and Ireland's biggest treehouse The three-story fairytale house is a gem that many people have travelled from all over the world to see. It has been included in prestigious publications such as the Irish Time and the Herald as a memorable adventure.
