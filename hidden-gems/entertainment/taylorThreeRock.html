Taylor’s Three Rock

General Information

Enjoy a night of Irish dancing, singing and “craic” at Ireland's most adored pub and entertainment restaurant. The entertainment at Taylor's Three Rock ranges from soulful ballads to energetic foot stepping music. You are sure to have a great time with the entertainment of the resident comedian Novel V. Ginnity.

Hidden Quirks

Taylor Three Rock has received many visits from prominent people from all corners of the world over the years. Novel V. Ginnity is sure to share a history lesson of the prominent historical individuals who fought in Irish battles as well as stories about South Africa's Nelson Mandela and Desmond Tutu who also paid a visit to this wholesome pub
