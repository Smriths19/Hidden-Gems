Cape Clear Island Distillery

General Information

Cape Clear Island Distillery is one of eight islands that speak Gaelic. It is on the southwest coast of County Cork and presents scenery that will undoubtedly take your breath away. This visit is set to be peaceful and wholesome as the population in the area is decreasing. Cape Clear Island Distillery has some of the best whiskey Ireland has to offer with an award winning gin called 3 SQ.miles. The gin beautifully blends the island-harvested honey-suckle, berries and lemon.
