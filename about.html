About Us

Hidden Gems was founded in 2023 by a team of Trinity College students, in which four out of the five are international students. Despite our different backgrounds, we all could agree that there weren't enough platforms that explored unique and unpopular Irish experiences even though these experiences were oftentimes intrinsic to Ireland. We all agreed that the “common and populated” Irish experiences were well marketed but were not always unique to the Irish experience.

Following a brainstorming session, Hidden Gems blossomed into a virtual home for quirky and cool adventure seekers. We are the friend in our user's pocket that is guiding them through finding spots that they will talk about years from their visit, meet like minded people and belong in a community of quirky and cool people.

At Hidden Gems, we are lucky to have individuals with comprehensive expertise that allow us to provide these unique experiences while promoting small local businesses who are in much need of consumer attention.

We encourage you to join us by submitting a unique spot you have come across, use the platform to find a gem that aligns with your interests, contribute to creating positive social impact and engage with our community.

See you soon.

Vision & Mission

Hidden Gems is a travel blog that aims to provide unique, authentic and quirky Irish experiences to tourists and locals who are seeking wholesome and unsaturated tourist destinations.

We recognise the beauty of belonging to a community. Thus we provide our community an opportunity to find like-minded people to explore a plethora of truly Irish experiences with.

Our platform aims to make adventures captivating, holistic and convenient. Whether it is your first time in Ireland or you are a local seeking for an adventure that awakens your soul, Hidden Gems is the friend you need in your pocket.
