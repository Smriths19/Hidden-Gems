Birr Castle Demesne

The Swiss Cottage, just outside the heritage town of Cahir, is a cottage built in the early 1800s by Richard Butler, 1st Earl of Glengall, who, we believe, managed to persuade world-famous Regency architect John Nash to design it. Originally, simply known as “The Cottage” it appears to have acquired its present name because it was thought to resemble an Alpine cottage. Inside, there is a graceful spiral staircase and some exquisitely decorated rooms. The wallpaper is partly original and partly the fruit of a 1980s restoration project, in which the renowned fashion designer Sybil Connolly was responsible for the interiors.

Hidden Quirks

The cottage has many quintessential rooms like the well-lit music room, small bedroom and large bedroom. If you are a history fan with a niche taste for quirky experiences. The Swiss Cottage is the perfect gem for you.
